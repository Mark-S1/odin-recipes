Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. This lasagna recipe is great because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.

Ingredients

  • 450g Italian sausage
  • 350g beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves crushed garlic
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 370g tomato sauce
  • 340g tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tbsp fresh parsley
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 340g mozzarella cheese
  • 450g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Cook beef, onion, and garlic in a large pot over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tbsp parsley, basil, 1 tsp salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Cover and simmer for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tbsp parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Preheat oven to 190 deg C
  6. To assemble, spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 23x33cm baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1.5 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with oil or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  9. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
